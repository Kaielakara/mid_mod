About MidMod

MidMod was created by designer Matt Downey as a way to collect and catalog his favorite mid-century modern pieces on Amazon. What started out as a passion project has turned in to a meticulously curated site featuring the best of mid-century modern design, decor , cocktails , music, and more.

Our inspiration comes from legends like Charles and Ray Eames , Florence Knoll , George Nelson , and Harry Bertoia. The admiration we have for these iconic designers is at the heart of each of our collections.

Custom & Curated

Hand-picked by designers

We scour Amazon for the best mid-century inspired products. Our designers choose pieces that marry form and function while staying true to the design sensibilities that inspire the era.

Trusted & Familiar

Source by Amazon

We've partnered with Amazon because they have great prices, fast shipping (especially with Prime), and excellent customer support. By cultivating our collections from their stock, we're able to bring you a simple and familiar shopping process that just works.
